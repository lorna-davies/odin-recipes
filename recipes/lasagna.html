Lasagna

Lasagna is an amazing Italian dish, made of alternating layers of bechamel sauce, bolognaise, and pasta sheets

You'll find the ingredients and the method you'll need below

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • beef mince (or veggie alternative)
  • tomato sauce
  • onion
  • beef or veg stock
  • lasagna pasta sheets

Steps

  1. Make bolognaise sauce
  2. Make bechamel sauce
  3. Blanch pasta sheets
  4. Assemble in layer
  5. Bake in oven
